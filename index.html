Profile

Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Cyprus University of Technology and Adjunct Lecturer at AUB Mediterraneo and Neapolis University Paphos. My work focuses on AI-driven resource optimization, cloud–edge orchestration, and blockchain systems. I lead research activities in EU-funded projects such as HYPER-AI, PHYSICS, and FAME, working on distributed intelligence, serverless computing, and trustworthy AI. Previously, I was a Senior Researcher at the University of Nicosia, where I contributed to blockchain infrastructure, benchmarking, and EU digital identity initiatives. I actively publish in reputable academic venues and enjoy bridging research with real-world applications, combining hands-on technical work and AI-based optimization frameworks.
